About this archive

What and why

For seventy years, NASA has redefined the boundaries of the possible, turning the unknown into knowledge. To celebrate this historic milestone, we challenged graphic and web designers from around the world to shape this legacy, creating digital platforms driven by their own artistic and methodological vision. This archive is the official repository of that global initiative—a dedicated space to showcase how the epic journey of space exploration inspires contemporary design. Through clean geometries, rigorous grids, and interactive storytelling, these projects honor NASA’s past while inspiring future generations to keep looking up.
